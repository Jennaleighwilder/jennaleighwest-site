Questions

Frequently Asked

Click any question to open the answer.

What kind of consulting do you offer?

I offer direct systems consulting — meaning I help you map, understand, and navigate whatever system you are stuck inside. That could be a legal system, a family system, a business system, a creative block, or an institutional process. I also offer software and AI development consulting for founders and builders.

How long does a software build take?

Simple landing pages and intake tools can move quickly. More custom software or AI builds take longer and depend on scope, revisions, and integrations. Every build starts with a scoping conversation so we can decide what is actually needed before naming a timeline.

How much does consulting cost?

A single Discovery Session is $150 for 60 minutes and gives you a full map with next steps. Full engagements start at $400 and are scoped to your situation. Software builds are quoted per project after an initial conversation. Digital products and reports are listed in the Shop and the Reports page.

Do you work with neurodivergent clients?

Yes — intentionally and specifically. I am neurodivergent myself. I work in a way that honors non-linear thinking, pattern recognition, and the kind of knowing that does not fit standard frameworks. If you have been told your mind works wrong, it probably works differently. That is not the same thing.

What is the Mirror Protocol?

The Mirror Protocol is the name I use for my pattern-reading framework. It shapes how I ask questions, track repeating themes, and build reflective tools that help people see themselves more clearly. On this site, it shows up most directly in the AI tools and in the way I structure reports.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes. I sign NDAs for all software development and sensitive consulting work. Confidentiality is standard, not an add-on. What you share with me stays with me.

What does the Bloodline Report include?

A personalized 20-30 page written report exploring your ancestral patterns — the gifts, wounds, and recurring themes carried in your bloodline. I draw from Appalachian heritage wisdom, cross-cultural tradition, and your intake answers. It is specific to you, not a template. Delivered within 7-10 days of your intake form submission.

Can you build AI-powered applications?

Yes — AI integration is a core part of my software work. I have built systems powered by Claude, OpenAI, and custom prompt architectures. From simple chatbots to complex multi-modal research engines, I build AI tools that actually do something specific rather than just existing.

Do you offer ongoing support after a project?

Yes. For software: I offer ongoing maintenance, updates, and support packages. For consulting: I offer follow-up sessions and check-ins. Nothing I build is meant to be handed off and forgotten — it is meant to work, and if it stops working, I want to know.

Where are you based? Do you work remotely?

I am based in rural Tennessee — fifth generation Appalachian. I work entirely remotely and serve clients across the US and internationally. Time zones are not a barrier. I respond within 24 hours on business days.

What systems do you decode — what does that mean exactly?

Any system — legal, medical, educational, financial, family, institutional, creative, spiritual, digital. A system is any set of rules, relationships, and pressures that shapes what you can and cannot do. I have traveled across prep schools, public housing, off-grid homesteads, and corporate institutions. I know how to read a room — and a system is just a room with rules written in invisible ink.

How do I get started?

Fill out the contact form on the Connect page, or email theforgottencode780@gmail.com directly. Tell me your name, what you are trying to move through or build, and your timeline. I will respond within 24 hours with next steps. If you are not sure what you need, the AI Tools page has a Service Advisor that can help you figure it out.
